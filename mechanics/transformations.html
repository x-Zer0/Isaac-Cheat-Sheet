Transformations

The Binding of Isaac Rebirth introduced a couple of transformations to the game, but the Afterbirth DLC comes packed with an additional 9 to play with and Afterbirth+ introduces 3 more.

A transformation gives you special abilities in addition to your current form and each one holds a unique effect to help you along your way. The criteria for each one is listed below and they all involve picking up a specific combination of items.

Guppy

Isaac turns into his cat Guppy and gains the ability to fly. Every time you successfully hit with a tear, a blue fly will spawn (In Repentance this was nerfed to 50% of tears that hit). Taking any Three of the following items will trigger the Guppy transformation.
